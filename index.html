A bit about me...

So,

I'm Anne - Freelance Web Designer with a Design/Fitness/Retail background. Careerwise, I have worn many hats, but my true love is designing art for natural fibers, materials, and abstract art using pattern. proportion, and harmony with the Adobe Creative Suite along with other software such as Figma. About three years ago I became intensely interested in mobile & web projects. I focused on templateless web design using simple typography, layouts, and animations as seen here. With my experience, I know how to solve real business cases by using design thinking. The inspiration for this site is mid-century colors, graphics, and typography. I wanted to create a work of art rather than a website with that cookie-cutter feel. This site is made from HTML CSS and Javascript!
